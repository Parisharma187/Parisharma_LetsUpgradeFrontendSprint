"Clarity Cards- A Mental Health Visual Tool"

💭 Why Clarity Cards?

✨Clarity Cards are gentle mood cards for those moments when speaking feels impossible. Whether you're feeling anxious, numb, overwhelmed- or just need someone to know - let a card say it for you.

Soft🤗, Simple😊, Silent🙂

" You don't always need to speak, Just Flip😎"

How to Use?

➡️Tap(on mobile📲) or hover(on desktop💻) over a card to reveal a gentle, grounding message. Use them to express how you're feeling when words feel stuck. These are not just cards- "They're quiet reflections of what you're feeling inside🥹."
